# The Christmann Group
## Sustainable Organic Synthesis | FU Berlin

Welcome to the research group of Prof. Dr. Mathias Christmann. Our lab focuses on natural product synthesis and the development of sustainable catalytic methods.

### Research Areas
* **Natural Product Synthesis:** Total synthesis of complex molecular frameworks.
* **Organocatalysis:** Developing new, eco-friendly catalytic transformations.
* **Electrosynthesis:** Utilizing electrons as reagents for modern synthesis.
